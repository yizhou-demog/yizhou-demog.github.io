## About Me

I am a tenured associate professor at the Guanghua School of Management and the Center for Social Research at Peking University.

## Research Interests

- **Economic Demography:** fertility, marriage, aging, household decision-making, population and economic change

- **Formal Demography:** population aging, fertility and mortality, demographic transition, population structure, demographic measurement

- **Quantitative Social Science:** causal inference, longitudinal data analysis, survey research, social networks, computational social science

- **Family, Aging, and Health:** intergenerational spillovers, elderly health, mental health, health behaviors, family disruption

- **Inequality and Social Change in China:** educational stratification, gender segregation, social mobility, redistribution attitudes, public opinion
